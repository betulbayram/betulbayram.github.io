Curriculum Development

For the AI Development Bootcamp, I designed a layered and scalable curriculum tailored to Junior, Junior+, Middle, Middle+, and Senior levels. The structure of topics, hands-on exercises, and project-based content were carefully adapted to match the skill levels of participants, providing a personalized and effective learning journey.

This curriculum reached over 800 students from more than 40 universities across Turkey, generating significant engagement and impact.
